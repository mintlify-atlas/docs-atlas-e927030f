# Bike Compatibility

SmartSpin2k is compatible with a wide range of indoor cycling bikes. This page lists supported bikes, required components, and fitment information.

## Compatibility Requirements

For SmartSpin2k to work with your bike, it must have:

1. **Manual Resistance Knob** - A physical knob that controls resistance
2. **Sufficient Clearance** - Space between knob and head tube (see clearance requirements below)
3. **Stable Mounting Location** - Head tube or frame suitable for mounting

<Note>
  SmartSpin2k does NOT work with bikes that have electronic resistance control only (like Peloton Bike+) or bikes without a physical resistance knob.
</Note>

## Clearance Requirements

Measure from the **back of the head tube** (side closest to cyclist) to the **center of the resistance knob**:

| Version/Case | Minimum Clearance | Best For |
|--------------|------------------|----------|
| **V3 Integrated PCB** | 80mm | Peloton, Bowflex C6, Echelon Connect Sport |
| **V2 Direct Mount Long** | 115mm | Bowflex C6, Schwinn IC4, Echelon |
| **V2 Direct Mount Short** | 81mm | Bowflex C7, compact bikes |
| **V2 Universal Mount** | 49mm | Wide range of bikes |
| **V2 Ultra Short Mod** | &lt;49mm | Very compact bikes |

### Custom Bike Mount

If your bike doesn't have a pre-made mount:

1. Measure the yoke width of your head tube
2. Use the [Thingiverse Customizer](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5738735)
3. Set YokeWidth parameter to your measurement
4. Adjust YokeLength if needed for your geometry
5. Export and print custom STL

**Example:** Yosuda Bike Plus uses 30mm yoke width

## Not Sure About Your Bike?

### Step 1: Check Requirements

- [ ] Does your bike have a **manual resistance knob**? (not electronic only)
- [ ] Can you measure **clearance** from head tube to knob center?
- [ ] Is there a **stable head tube** or frame for mounting?

### Step 2: Measure Your Bike

1. **Clearance:** Measure from back of head tube to center of knob
2. **Knob Diameter:** Measure your resistance knob diameter and depth
3. **Head Tube Width:** Measure yoke width if using direct mount

### Step 3: Find Your Parts

1. **Check Insert List** - Look for your bike model or similar knob size
2. **Check Arm Table** - Find bikes with similar clearance
3. **Choose Case Version** - Based on clearance measurement

### Step 4: Ask the Community

- [Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/716297469953492/) - Search for your bike model
- Someone may have already built for your exact bike
- Share measurements and photos for help
- Community members can suggest similar bikes

## Incompatible Bikes

<Warning>
  These bikes are NOT compatible with SmartSpin2k:
</Warning>

- **Peloton Bike+** - Electronic resistance only, no manual knob
- **NordicTrack S22i/S15i** - Electronic resistance only
- **Echelon EX-8S** - Electronic resistance (older Echelon models with manual knob work)
- **Any bike without a manual resistance knob**
- **Bikes with magnetic resistance controlled electronically only**

### Why Electronic Resistance Bikes Don't Work

SmartSpin2k physically turns a resistance knob. Bikes with purely electronic resistance have no knob to turn, making SmartSpin2k incompatible.

## Resistance Knob Compatibility

SmartSpin2k works with these knob types:

- ✅ **Friction knob** - Turn to increase/decrease pad pressure
- ✅ **Magnetic knob** - Turn to move magnets closer/farther
- ✅ **Combination** - Both friction and magnetic
- ✅ **Spherical knobs** - Direct Mount provides better stability
- ✅ **Cylindrical knobs** - Universal or Direct Mount both work
- ❌ **Electronic buttons/switches only** - Not compatible
