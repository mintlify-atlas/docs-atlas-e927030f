### Total Filament Estimate

- **Complete V3 Build:** ~400-500g rigid + 50g TPU
- **Complete V2 Direct Mount:** ~450-550g rigid + 50g TPU
- **Complete V2 Universal:** ~350-400g rigid + 50g TPU

## Sourcing Recommendations

### Electronics Suppliers

<Accordion title="United States">
  - **Amazon** - Fast shipping, ESP32 devkits, stepper motors
  - **DigiKey** - Professional components, exact specs
  - **Mouser** - Alternative to DigiKey
  - **AliExpress** - Budget components (longer shipping)
  - **Adafruit** - Quality devkits and drivers
  - **SparkFun** - Educational-grade components
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Europe">
  - **RS Components** - Professional supplier
  - **Farnell** - Wide component selection
  - **AliExpress** - Budget option
  - **Amazon (local)** - Fast delivery
  - **TME** - Eastern Europe specialist
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Asia Pacific">
  - **AliExpress** - Best prices, local shipping
  - **Taobao** - China domestic
  - **Element14** - Professional components
  - **RS Components** - Australia/NZ
</Accordion>

### PCB Manufacturing

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="V3 PCB">
    **Pre-assembled (Recommended):**
    - [SmartSpin2k.com Kits](https://www.smartspin2k.com/purchase-kits)
    
    **DIY Manufacturing:**
    - **JLCPCB** - SMT assembly service
    - **PCBWay** - Alternative SMT service
    - **Seeed Studio** - Fusion PCB service
    
    <Warning>
      V3 requires SMT assembly. Upload BOM and CPL files carefully.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="V2 PCB">
    **Direct Order:**
    - [PCBWay Project Link](https://www.pcbway.com/project/shareproject/SmartSpin2k_PCB.html)
    
    **Custom Order:**
    - Upload Gerber files from Hardware/V2/PCB/
    - Any PCB manufacturer (JLCPCB, OSH Park, etc.)
    - Specifications: 1.6mm, HASL finish
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
